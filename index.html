People see credit cards as toxic and tune out when banks try to 'educate' them. I helped Barclaycard overcome this by using the language of pop culture to coach the nation towards credit confidence, turning a topic people actively ignore into something genuinely engaging.

To become the most entertaining brand in women’s football, Pepsi Max had to avoid following category conventions that positioned it as the same as the men's game. I defined a credible role for the brand by focusing on what makes the women's game uniquely entertaining: the outsized importance of skill and dribbles.
