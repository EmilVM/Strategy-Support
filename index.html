Cases

I helped reposition Lyca Mobile as the smart choice in a price-obsessed telco market

Problem: Lyca Mobile wanted to relaunch as a subscription-first brand in a saturated, price-obsessed telco market. But with low awareness and a legacy as a kiosk SIM provider, they needed more than just another cheap offer: they needed a clear, ownable point of view.

Insight: With mobile data, a higher price doesn’t mean a better experience. Fake news remain fake news, and your Tinder game won’t improve just because you chose an expensive mobile subscription.

Strategy: Reframe low cost from dodgy to smart by showing that with Lyca Mobile, you pay less for what you love (and what you love hating).
